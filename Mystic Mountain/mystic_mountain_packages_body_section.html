Experience the magic of Mystic Mountain in Ocho Rios, where Jamaican tales and adventure unite. Beyond an ordinary adventure park, Mystic Mountain immerses you in Jamaica’s vibrant culture and breathtaking nature. From the exhilarating Bobsled ride, to the serene Hummingbird Garden showcasing the island’s natural wonders, each attraction unfolds a chapter of Jamaica’s story.

Join us for an adventure filled with excitement and lasting memories!

Explore our thrilling activities, inspired by the island’s stories, and choose from four tour packages – Mystic Silver, Mystic Gold, Mystic Platinum, and Mystic Adventure Pass – for an unforgettable journey into the heart of Jamaica. Whether you’re soaring on a zipline or ascending the mountain on the Sky Explorer, every moment promises a memorable experience.
